Privacy Policy

Last updated · 4 June 2026

The Vital Years respects your privacy. This page explains the limited information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and the choices you have.

The Vital Years ("The Vital Years," "we," "our," or "us") publishes the website at laappflex.shop (the "Site"), a personal editorial journal by Giulia Batista on breathing, nerve health, and aging well. We are an editorial publication, not a store or a clinic. We do not process payments, ship products, or keep medical records, so the information we collect is small.

I.Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Correspondence: if you write to us at giulia@laappflex.shop, we receive your email address, your name (if included), and the contents of your message.
  • Subscription (if offered): if and when we offer an email newsletter, we collect your email address and any preferences you provide. Subscription is always optional.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and browser information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, language preference, and time zone.
  • Usage information: pages viewed, time spent on pages, and other interactions with the Site.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: see Section IV.

1.3 Information from third parties

We may receive aggregated information about our readers from analytics providers and the platforms on which we promote our letters (such as Meta and Google). This helps us understand which letters resonate.

II.How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to your correspondence personally.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Site and the journal.
  • Understand which letters readers return to and value most.
  • Send email communications you have explicitly opted in to receive (you may opt out at any time).
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unlawful use of the Site.
  • Comply with our legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

III.How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information for monetary consideration. We share information only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers: trusted vendors who help us operate the Site, such as web hosting (Cloudflare), email delivery, and analytics. They may only use your information for the purposes we authorize.
  • Advertising and analytics partners: Meta (Facebook), Google Analytics, and similar platforms, to measure how readers find our letters. See Section IV for opt-out options.
  • Legal compliance: when required by law, subpoena, court order, a lawful request from a public authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of The Vital Years, our readers, or others.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of the publication, subject to the protections of this Policy.
  • With your consent: in any other case where you have explicitly consented.

IV.Cookies and Tracking

We and our third-party partners use cookies, web beacons, pixels (including the Meta Pixel and Google Analytics tags), and similar technologies to recognize your device, remember your preferences, analyze readership, and measure the reach of our letters.

Custom fonts on this Site are served via fonts.bunny.net, a GDPR-compliant proxy of the Google Fonts library that does not transmit your IP address to Google.

You can manage cookie preferences through:

  • Your browser settings (most browsers let you block or delete cookies).
  • Digital Advertising Alliance: optout.aboutads.info
  • Network Advertising Initiative: optout.networkadvertising.org
  • European readers: youronlinechoices.com

V.Your Privacy Rights

5.1 General rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your information, to object to processing, to withdraw consent, to request portability, and to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority. To exercise any of these rights, email giulia@laappflex.shop.

5.2 California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights, including the right to know what categories of personal information we collect, to request deletion, to correct inaccurate information, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information. We do not sell personal information for money. However, the use of certain advertising cookies (such as the Meta Pixel) may be considered a "sale" or "share" under California law. To opt out, email giulia@laappflex.shop with the subject line "Do Not Sell or Share, California" and we will honor your request. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

5.3 European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland

If you are located in the EEA, the UK, or Switzerland, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and equivalent laws apply. The legal bases on which we process your information include performance of a contract (such as responding to your correspondence), compliance with legal obligations, our legitimate interests in operating the publication balanced against your rights, and your consent where required. You may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

VI.Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Reader correspondence is typically retained for up to three years for continuity in our editorial relationship with you.

VII.Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

VIII.Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal information should contact giulia@laappflex.shop.

IX.International Data Transfers

Our operations are based in the United States. If you access the Site from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country. By using the Site, you consent to such transfers where permitted by applicable law.

X.Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites or services that we do not operate. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any services you visit.

XI.Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version is always posted on this page with the "Last updated" date above. If we make material changes, we will provide additional notice as required by law.
